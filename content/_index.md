{{< textblock heading="Services" linkref="services" background=true  >}}

  {{< textp gold=true >}}
  Our cèilidh package is perfect for all kinds of events: weddings, parties, charity evenings, corporate events or student nights. A typical cèilidh package includes a four hour hire of our three-piece band (including a break), dance calling, and a disco set. We have a wide repertoire of cèilidh dances, including old favourites like the Dashing White Sergeant, and less well-known options like the Pat-a-Cake Polka. To ensure that everyone has a chance to get up and dance, we perform a mixture of fast, slow, couples, and group dances. 
  {{< /textp >}}

  {{< textp gold=true >}}
  We are always happy to tailor your cèilidh to suit your specific event, however here are a few examples:
  {{< /textp >}}

  {{< tabs gold=true >}}

      {{< tab label="Wedding" >}}
      Example programme:
      - 6:30 - 8pm: Band arrives, set up and soundchecks
      - 8 - 9.30pm: First dance then into cèilidh set
      - 9.30 - 10.15pm: Break for evening buffet
      - 10.15 - 11pm: More cèilidh dances
      - 11pm - midnight: Disco
      - Last dance: Auld Lang Syne/Loch Lomond
      {{< /tab >}}

      {{< tab label="Party" >}}

      - 9:45 - 10:15pm: Disco

{{< textblock heading="FAQs" linkref="faqs" background=false  >}}

  {{< accordion >}}
    {{< accordionItem title="Should I have a cèilidh?" >}}
    Absolutely! A cèilidh is a great way to get friends and family up on the dance floor and enjoying themselves. Don't worry if your guests have never been to a ceilidh before, we call out every dance and can even demonstrate if needed. We will also provided a DJ set in our breaks, so you will be getting the best of both. 
    {{< /accordionItem >}}

    {{< accordionItem title="How much do you cost?" >}}
    Every gig is unique. Once you get in touch and provide us with a few details, we will send you a quote for your event. 
    {{< /accordionItem >}}

    {{< accordionItem title="How long do you play for?" >}}
    We usually provided a 4 hour set, which includes a mixture of cèilidh and disco. However every event is unique and we can tailor this to suit your needs. 
    {{< /accordionItem >}}

    {{< accordionItem title="What time will you arrive, and how long does set up take?" >}}
    We will arrive approximately 90 mins before we are scheduled to play, to set up and soundcheck. 
    {{< /accordionItem >}}

    {{< accordionItem title="What can you do for my first dance?" >}}
    For your first dance, we offer a range of options to make it truly special! You could choose to have us play any of our ceilidh dances, many couples choose a classic Waltz, or we even had one couple start with a Strip the Willow! Alternatively, we can perform a Scottish song live for you – our personal favourite was an arrangement of Wild Mountain Thyme which we played with two violins! Or if you would prefer not to have something played live you can choose any song that we can play through our PA speaker system.  
    {{< /accordionItem >}}

    {{< accordionItem title="What happens if a band member is unwell and unable to attend my event?" >}}
    If a band member becomes unwell, we will strive to do our best to provide the same high quality service that you booked. We have a number of backup musicians that can step in should this happen.
    {{< /accordionItem >}}

    {{< accordionItem title="How do I pay?" >}}
    We accept bank transfer, cheque or cash.
    {{< /accordionItem >}}

    {{< accordionItem title="What happens if I have to cancel my booking?" >}}
    In the unfortunate event that you need to cancel your event, please get in touch with us as soon as possible. Our cancelation policy is:

    {{< textp >}}
    - Any time over 4 weeks - 20% deposit
    {{< /textp >}}
    {{< textp >}}
    - Less than 4 weeks before event - 50% of fee
    {{< /textp >}}
    {{< textp >}}
    - Less than 2 weeks before event - 100% of fee
    {{< /textp >}}
    {{< /accordionItem >}}

    {{< accordionItem title="What happens in your disco set?" >}}
    We provide a disco set during our break and then often to finish off the evening. How much disco can be decided by the client. We always ask our clients for a list of songs that they would like to hear on the night, and then we use this list to tailor the disco to suit their tastes. 
    {{< /accordionItem >}}
  {{< /accordion >}}
